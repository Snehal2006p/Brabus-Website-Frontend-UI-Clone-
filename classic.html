For the love of craft

The beauty is brought back to life with the utmost precision and conscientious dedication: from complete chemical paint stripping to careful engine and gearbox overhaul, from elaborate interior renovation, cathodic dip coating of the bodywork as in modern production vehicles and comprehensive rust prevention to the final paint job. Once all the work steps have been completed, the classic Mercedes shines again in its former glory.

Grade 1+

After the completed BRABUS Classic 6-star restoration, the leading classic car expert organization Classic Data rates the vehicle with the grade 1+ certificate. The vehicle receives the additional certificate "New Car Character" for the unique quality and fidelity to the original work: The car is like new - the 6-star restoration exceeds the original factory delivery condition in terms of quality of workmanship and rust prevention. A classic that can only be found in this quality at BRABUS Classic.

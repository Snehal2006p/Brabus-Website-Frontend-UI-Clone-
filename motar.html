LUXURY BEYOND PLACES

The extravagant motorhome, boasting a length of 12 meters (39.4 ft) and over 30 square meters (320 sq ft) of refined, luxurious BRABUS Masterpiece living space, features two electrically extendable slide-outs on each side, which can extend the bedroom and saloon to a width of 4.50 meters (14.8 ft). Hydraulically extendable supports level the vehicle perfectly, ensuring that the BRABUS BIG BOY 1200 stands securely on any surface. A door with electrically extendable steps on the right-hand side of the saloon facilitates entry and exit to the passenger cabin.


Two extendable modules in the sleeping compartment create space for the comfortable, transversely positioned double bed measuring 160 x 200 centimeters (63 x 79 in). A large closet is integrated into the rear wall of the motorhome. A desk and a 43-inch 4K television are installed in the side wall opposite the bed, which, like an identical device in the saloon, can be used to watch TV programs or play games on the integrated Playstation 5 system. Thanks to satellite internet via Starlink, passengers can watch streaming services, access content from the internet or communicate with the whole world, even in remote areas.

In the living room, owners and their guests find comfortable seating on the two sofas upholstered in the finest gray BRABUS leather, which, like the seats in the driver's compartment and the door panels, are quilted with pinpoint precision in the BRABUS "Triangle" design. As a tasteful contrast to the gray used not only for parts of the interior but also for the high-quality carpeting, other sections are finished in black fine wood or in matching black Alcantara. Carbon trims add a decidedly sporty touch throughout the interior.


The saloon also features a modern kitchen with an induction hob, oven, dishwasher and spacious fridge with an integrated freezer compartment. A separate wine fridge and coffee machine are also available for drinks. The sofa opposite the kitchen, together with designer chairs upholstered in black and gray fabric and a large black table, can become part of a dining room. This ensemble can also be used as a comfortable workstation or a mobile office. Of course, there is also a luxurious bathroom in fine natural stone with a rainforest shower, WC and washbasin between the bedroom and living area.

An automatic air conditioning system and a parking heater ensure comfortable temperatures throughout the motorhome, depending on the season and requirements. Wherever the BRABUS BIG BOY 1200 is parked, both systems can be plugged in or powered on the move by the eight solar panels on the roof and the on-board energy storage system.

From the outside, the BRABUS BIG BOY 1200 impresses with its king-size dimensions and elegant all-black exterior. The brand-typical look also includes the illuminated BRABUS logo in the exposed carbon radiator grille. The same high-tech compound is used to produce the side wind deflectors, the covers for the fog lights and the handles for opening the hood. To match the main headlights, the auxiliary headlights attached to the BRABUS roof light bar and the rear lights likewise feature LED technology.


Of course, an exclusive BRABUS motorhome also needs a powerful engine. The 12.8-liter six-cylinder turbodiesel delivers 390 kW / 530 hp and generates a maximum torque of 2,600 Nm (1,918 lb-ft). The BRABUS BIG BOY 1200‚Äôs top speed is limited to 90 km/h (56 mph).
